Clawson Public Schools is a school district in Michigan, headquartered in Clawson in Metro Detroit.
The Madison District Public Schools of Madison Heights contracts its busing services to Clawson. The Madison District did so in order to save money. Paul Rogers, the superintendent of the Madison District, stated in 2008 that there were annual savings of $165,000 for contracting with Clawson.
