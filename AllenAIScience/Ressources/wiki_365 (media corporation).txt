365 is a mass media company in Iceland, which produces eight TV stations, five radio stations, one newspaper and a website.
365 also rebroadcasts foreign television channels over its digital system. It started broadcasting Bylgjan in 1986, Stöð 2 started in 1986, Stöð 2 Sport (Sýn) in 1995, Bíórásin in 1998, and it started publishing Fréttablaðið in 2001.
