The 4 Seasons Greetings is the second studio album by The Four Seasons. It was released in 1962 on Vee-Jay Records as a monophonic recording and later again the same year in stereo.
Side one features traditional carols, while the second side contains their contemporary, rock and pop flourishes. The album was reissued in 1966 on Philips Records with a new title (The 4 Seasons' Christmas Album) and different cover artwork.
