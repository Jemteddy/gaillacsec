Collapse 12 Floors is an album by the Japanese noise musician Merzbow. It references the collapse of the Ryōunkaku tower during the 1923 Great Kantō earthquake.


== Track listing ==
All music composed by Masami Akita.


== Personnel ==
Masami Akita – Apple computer, theremin, noise electronix
Abtechtronix and Merzbow – artworks
Killer – layout
