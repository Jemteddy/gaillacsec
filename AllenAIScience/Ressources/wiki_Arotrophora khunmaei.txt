Arotrophora khunmaei is a species of moth of the Tortricidae family. It is found in Thailand.
The wingspan is about 18 mm. The ground colour of forewings is greyish brown, dotted with black and with brown markings, as well as black strigulae along the costa and a row of dots along the termen. The hindwings are brownish.


== Etymology ==
The species name refers to Khun Mae Ngaay, the type locality.
