The Invisible Man is the sixth solo album by American Music Club singer/songwriter Mark Eitzel, released by Matador Records in 2001. It is a more electronic effort by Eitzel, who is known for his more stark, acoustic arrangements, and was allegedly recorded on a Macintosh computer in his living room.
