Vrontou (Greek: Βροντού) is a town of the former Municipality of Dio, which is part of the municipality of Dio-Olympos, in the Pieria regional unit, Central Macedonia, Greece, at an altitude of 120 meters, with 2,081 inhabitants (census 2001). There are several medieval and post-medieval churches in Vrontou.
An impressive and spectacular event is the carnival of Vrontou,, that take place every year.
