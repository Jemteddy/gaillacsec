The Măneşti-Vlădeni gas field natural gas field in Măneşti near Vlădeni in Dâmboviţa County. It was discovered in 1943 and developed by Petrom. It began production in 1945 and produces natural gas and condensates. The total proven reserves of the Măneşti-Vlădeni gas field are around 52 billion cubic feet (1.5 km³), and production is slated to be around 4.6 million cubic feet/day (0.13×105m³) in 2008.
