Lander Crossing (also, Lander) is an unincorporated community in Placer County, California. Lander Crossing is located on the Southern Pacific Railroad, 2.5 miles (4.0 km) south-southwest of Colfax. It lies at an elevation of 2300 feet (701 m).
The name honors Frederick West Lander, superintendent for the wagon road in 1858.
