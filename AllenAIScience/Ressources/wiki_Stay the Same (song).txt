"Stay the Same" is a song by Gabrielle and the first single from her fourth studio album Play to Win. The single managed to peak at number twenty in the UK Singles Chart, helping the album peak inside the UK top ten. "Stay the Same" was, however, her lowest charting single in eight years, albeit her tenth consecutive top twenty hit.
