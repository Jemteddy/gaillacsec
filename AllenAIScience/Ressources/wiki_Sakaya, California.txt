Sakaya (also Saccaya or Sak-ka-ya) is a former Sierra Miwok or Mono Native American settlement within the Sierra Nevada mountains of Mariposa County, California, United States.
Sakaya was located west of Sentinel Rock near the Merced River in Yosemite Valley, in present-day Yosemite National Park .
