== Taxonomy ==
The Acroreiidae family is classified in the informal group, Basommatophora (according to the taxonomy of the Gastropoda by Bouchet & Rocroi, 2005).
The family Acroreiidae has no subfamilies.


== Genera ==
The type genus is Acroreia Cossmann, 1885
