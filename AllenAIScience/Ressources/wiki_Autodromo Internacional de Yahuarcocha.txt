Autodromo Internacional de Yahuarcocha, also known as the Autódromo José Tobar Tobar, is a motorsport circuit located in Ibarra, Ecuador, around the lake Yawarkucha. It has hosted events in the Formula Renault series. It opened in 1970.


== Autódromo Mayor ==
This track is currently inactive. The layout is around 10 km.


== Autódromo José Tobar Tobar ==

The short course is 3.59 km long.
