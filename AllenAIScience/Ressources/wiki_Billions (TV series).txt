Billions is an upcoming American television drama series starring Damian Lewis and Paul Giamatti. The series was ordered by Showtime in March 2015 with a 12-episode pick up and will premiere on January 17, 2016.


== CastEdit ==
Damian Lewis as Bobby "Axe" Axelrod, an ambitious hedge fund king
Paul Giamatti as U.S. Attorney Chuck Rhoades
Maggie Siff as Wendy Rhoades
Malin Åkerman as Lara Axelrod
Toby Leonard Moore as Bryan Connerty
David Costabile as Mike "Wags" Wagner
Kelly AuCoin as Bill "Dollar" Stearn
Condola Rashād as Kate Sacher
