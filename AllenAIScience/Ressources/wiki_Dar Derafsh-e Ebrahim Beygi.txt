Dar Derafsh-e Ebrahim Beygi (Persian: داردرفش ابراهيم بيگي‎‎, also Romanized as Dār Derafsh-e Ebrāhīm Beygī) is a village in Baladarband Rural District, in the Central District of Kermanshah County, Kermanshah Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 130, in 25 families.
