This is a list of the main characters from the Canadian teen-drama series Degrassi: The Next Generation, renamed Degrassi from the tenth season till the fourteenth. This list is in alphabetical order by the character's first name.


== Main charactersEdit ==
The following actors have all received star billing and appeared in the opening credits of Degrassi: The Next Generation.
A regular is an actor who has appeared in the opening credits of the show in any given season. From Season 13-14, actors are only credited for the episodes they appear in.
A recurring status is given to actors who do not appear in the opening credits, but appear in several episodes of any particular season.
A guest appearance by an actor means they do not appear in the opening credits, but were guest starring in no more than two episodes of any particular season.
