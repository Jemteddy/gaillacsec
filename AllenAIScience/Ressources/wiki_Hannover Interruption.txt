Hannover Interruption (干渉ハノーバー, Kanshō Hannover) is an album by the Japanese noise musician Merzbow. It was released by Dradomel, a joint venture of Dragnet Records and Dom Elchklang. All the sleeve text is written in Japanese.


== Track listing ==


== Notes ==
Equipment used: Iron, aluminum, motor, mixer/feedback loop, various effects, tape, scratched compact disc, relaxation trainer, spring, contact mic, etc.
Side one includes live material recorded September 18, 1990 at Lowell University auditorium
Support: Reiko Azuma, Ron, Due Process, Achim Flaam


== Personnel ==
Merzbow – performer, editing
Abtechtonics – design
