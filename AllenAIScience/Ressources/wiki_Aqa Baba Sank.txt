Aqa Baba Sank (Persian: اقاباباسنك‎‎, also Romanized as Āqā Bābā Sank; also known as Āqā Bābā Sang and Āqā Bābāy Sang) is a village in Ozomdel-e Shomali Rural District, in the Central District of Varzaqan County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 487, in 103 families.
