Contemporary Clinical Trials is a bimonthly peer-reviewed medical journal covering clinical trials and research design in clinical medicine. It was established in 2005 and is published by Elsevier. The editor-in-chief is Zheng Su (Deerfield Institute). it was established in 1980 as Controlled Clinical Trials and obtained its current title in 2005.
According to the Journal Citation Reports, the journal has a 2014 impact factor of 1.935.
