The Hyderabad BRTS is a proposed transportation scheme for the Indian city of Hyderabad, India. Two corridors have been identified for testing the BRT System. Volvo Articulated Buses are being proposed to be used on the two corridors.


== Corridors ==
The following two corridors were identified for BRTS by the TSRTC and GHMC.
Corridor 1: Mehdipatnam — Gachibowli Crossroads — Botanical Gardens — Kothaguda Crossroads — MiyapurCorridor 2: Mehdipatnam — Malaysian Township — JNTU
