An octamer transcription factor is a transcription factor which binds to the "ATTTGCAT" sequence.
Examples include:
Oct-1 - POU2F1
Oct-2 - POU2F2
Oct-3/4 – POU5F1
Oct-6 – POU3F1
Oct-7 – POU3F2
Oct-8 - POU3F3
Oct-9 – POU3F4
Oct-11 – POU3F4
