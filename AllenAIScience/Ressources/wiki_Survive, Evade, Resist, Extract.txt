In the United Kingdom, SERE is an acronym for Survive, Evade, Resist and Extract. At a basic level, this is a core aspect of training for all UK Regular Army personnel and is tested as part of their Military Annual Training Tests (MATTs). The UK SERE School is based at RAF St Mawgan.
