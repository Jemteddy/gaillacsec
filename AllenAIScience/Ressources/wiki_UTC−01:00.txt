UTC−01:00 is an identifier for a time offset from UTC of −01. This time is used in:


== As standard time (Northern Hemisphere winter) ==


=== North America ===
Greenland
Eastern Greenland (Ittoqqortoormiit and surrounding area – observes European Union DST rules)


=== Atlantic Ocean ===
Portugal
Azores since 1966, except in 1992–1993 winter (WET/UTC+00:00 used instead)


== As standard time (all year round) ==


=== Atlantic Ocean ===
Cape Verde
