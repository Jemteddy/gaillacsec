Cambrian Heights is a residential neighbourhood in the north-west quadrant of Calgary, Alberta. It is located immediately south-east from Nose Hill Park, east of 14th Street NW and south of John Laurie Boulevard.


== Demographics ==
In the City of Calgary's 2012 municipal census, Cambrian Heights had a population of 7003205900000000000♠2,059 living in 7002930000000000000♠930 dwellings, a 1% increase from its 2011 population of 7003203900000000000♠2,039. With a land area of 0.9 km2 (0.35 sq mi), it had a population density of 2,290/km2 (5,900/sq mi) in 2012.
Residents in this community had a median household income of $59,522 in 2000, and there were 14.3% low income residents living in the neighbourhood. As of 2000, 12.8% of the residents were immigrants. A proportion of 10.6% of the buildings were condominiums or apartments, and 33.5% of the housing was used for renting.
