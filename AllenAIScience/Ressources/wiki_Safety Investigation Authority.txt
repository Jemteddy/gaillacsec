The Safety Investigation Authority of Finland (SIAF, Finnish: Onnettomuustutkintakeskus "Accident Investigation Center", OTKES; Swedish: Olycksutredningscentralen) is the accident investigation authority of Finland. It investigates all major accidents, and all aviation, maritime, and rail accidents and incidents. The board is located within the Ministry of Justice, and it is headquartered in Helsinki, the capital of Finland.
In English it was previously known as the Accident Investigation Board of Finland.
