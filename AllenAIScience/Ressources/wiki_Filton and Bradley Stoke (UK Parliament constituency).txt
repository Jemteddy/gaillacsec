Filton and Bradley Stoke is a constituency represented in the House of Commons of the UK Parliament. Its MP is Jack Lopresti, a Conservative.


== History ==
The seat was created from the Boundary Commission 2010 general election. The seat was formed by taking parts of the Bristol North West, Kingswood constituencies, and Northavon constituency.
The electoral wards used to create the seat in time for the 2010 election were:
Almondsbury, Bradley Stoke South, Bradley Stoke Central & Stoke Lodge, Bradley Stoke North, Downend, Filton, Frenchay & Stoke Park, Patchway, Pilning and Severn Beach, Staple Hill, Stoke Gifford and Winterbourne all in the South Gloucestershire (unitary) district
