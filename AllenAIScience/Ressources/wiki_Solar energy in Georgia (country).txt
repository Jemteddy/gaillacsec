Solar energy in Georgia is widely available, due to high average insolation. There are four methods of utilizing solar energy. Photovoltaics and concentrated solar power are methods of producing electricity from solar energy. Solar hot water is used to heat water using solar panels. Solar energy can also used for cooking in a solar oven.
