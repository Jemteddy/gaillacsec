Dry shampoo is a type of shampoo which reduces hair greasiness without the need for water. It is in powder form. It is typically administered from an aerosol can. Reported use in the United Kingdom estimate that 17% of women have used dry shampoo. Dry Shampoo is often based on corn starch or rice starch. Some contest that daily wash-and-rinse with detergent shampoo can strip away natural oil from hair.
