Mike and Ike Zours are a sour candy manufactured by Just Born. They are similar in shape to Mike and Ike candies. They were introduced to Just Born's line of candy in 1999, modeled after the Mike and Ikes which debuted in 1940. They are available in 5 oz "movie size" boxes, 4.2 oz boxes and 1.8 oz plastic bags. Zours are Mike and Ike Tangy Twister flavor with a sour coating.
They come in two unique blends:
ZOURS Original Fruitz
ZOURS Punch Fruitz (Discontinued)
