Megalomys is a genus of rodent in the family Cricetidae, part of the tribe Oryzomyini. The genus contains five large rodents from various Caribbean islands, all of which are now extinct. The last species to survive was M. desmarestii from Martinique, which went extinct after the Mount Pelée eruption in 1902.
It contains the following species:
Megalomys audreyae (Barbuda)
Megalomys curazensis (Curaçao)
Megalomys desmarestii (Martinique)
Megalomys luciae (Saint Lucia)
Megalomys georginae (Barbados)
