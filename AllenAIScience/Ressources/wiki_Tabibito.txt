"Tabibito" (旅人, Travelers) was the fourteenth single by the Japanese band The Blue Hearts and reached #50 on the Oricon charts in 1992. It was part of the band's sixth album, Stick Out.


== Details ==
The B-side track, "Taifū" (台風 Typhoon), is one of the rare songs by The Blue Hearts that uses irregular meter.
