Kilmore is a housing area of Dublin, Ireland, bordering the towns of Coolock, Santry and Beaumont. A smaller area which is part of the larger area is Kilmore West. The area is mostly housing estates, although there are public facilities such as schools, parks, shops and community centers. It is a social housing neighbourhood.


== Location and details ==
The town is situated in an area bordering the towns of Coolock to the east and north, Santry to the west and Beaumont and Artane to the south. It is on the Dublin 17/5 postal code region. There are three housing estates in Kimore, they are Cromcastle, Castletimon and Lorcan.
