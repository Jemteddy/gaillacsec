Only on the Left Side is the tenth studio album by rapper Daz Dillinger, released on August 12, 2008. In had been initially scheduled for an April or May release, but setbacks pushed the release back to August. The album debuted at number 38 on the Billboard R&B albums chart with 2,132 copies sold.


== SongsEdit ==
In addition to "Meal Ticket", Daz has also released other songs that will be on the album, including the title track, "Only On The Left Side", which samples the Snoop Dogg song Drop It Like It's Hot. He has also released "Thiz Weekend Again" featuring Soopafly. On March 26, 2008 he released his "street single", called "I'm Da Dopeman".
