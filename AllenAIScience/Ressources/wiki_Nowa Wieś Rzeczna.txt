Nowa Wieś Rzeczna [ˈnɔva ˈvjɛɕ ˈʐɛt͡ʂna] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Starogard Gdański, within Starogard County, Pomeranian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) west of Starogard Gdański and 46 km (29 mi) south of the regional capital Gdańsk.
For details of the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.
The village has a population of 685.
