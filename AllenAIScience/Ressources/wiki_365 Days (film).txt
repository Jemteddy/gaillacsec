365 Days is a 2015 Telugu, romance film written and directed by Ram Gopal Varma. Produced by D. Venkatesh, with soundtrack by Nag Sri Vatsa, the film stars Anaika Soti. Upon release, the film received mixed reviews.


== PlotEdit ==
The plot deals with newly wed couple and the problems faced by them.
