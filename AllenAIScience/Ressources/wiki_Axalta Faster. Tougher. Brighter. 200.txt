The Axalta 200 is a NASCAR Xfinity Series race held at Phoenix International Raceway in Avondale, Arizona. Bashas' Supermarkets sponsored the November race from 2002 until 2004. In 2005, they moved their sponsorship to the second Phoenix race added to the schedule in April.


== Past winners ==
2006, 2008, & 2009: Race extended due to a green–white–checker finish.
2011: Kyle Busch led all 200 laps from the pole to win, the first time it happened since 2003.
2014: Race was shortened due to rain.
