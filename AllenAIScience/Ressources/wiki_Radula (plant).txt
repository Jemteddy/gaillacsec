Radula is a genus of liverwort, and is the only genus in family Radulaceae.
Species include:
Radula complanata
Radula demissa M.A.M.Renner, 2013
Radula jonesii Bouman, Dirkse & Yamada
Radula kojana
Radula laxiramea
Radula marginata
Radula perrottetii
Radula visiniaca C. Massal.
