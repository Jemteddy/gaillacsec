New Hampshire's 5th State Senate District is one of 24 Senate districts. It is currently represented by Democrat David Pierce of Etna.


== District profile ==
The 5th district includes the southwestern portion of Grafton County and the westernmost communities of Sullivan County. The largest population center in the district is the city of Lebanon.
