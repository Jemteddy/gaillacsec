Sudani is the mobile arm of Sudatel Group (STG) of Companies in Sudan. Sudani is a mobile and broadband service provider to consumers and businesses in Sudan.
STG is a regional telecommunication provider with presence in four African countries.
Sudani is the first telecommunication company in Sudan to deploy 3.75G named as SudaniOne.


== OneConnect ==
Sudani OneConnect offers wireless internet connections, based on 3.75G technology, whether using a mobile or laptop, with speeds up to 7.2 Mbit/s.
