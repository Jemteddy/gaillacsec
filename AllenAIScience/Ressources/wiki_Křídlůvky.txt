Křídlůvky is a village and municipality (obec) in Znojmo District in the South Moravian Region of the Czech Republic.
The municipality covers an area of 7.87 square kilometres (3.04 sq mi), and has a population of 206 (as at 28 August 2006).
Křídlůvky lies approximately 17 kilometres (11 mi) east of Znojmo, 53 km (33 mi) south-west of Brno, and 195 km (121 mi) south-east of Prague.
