Goniatitina is one of two suborders included in the order Goniatitida; extinct Paleozoic ammonoid cephalopods only distantly related to the Nautiloidea.


== Taxonomy ==
The Goniatitina contains 17 defined superfamilies, listed below.
Adrianitaceae
Cyclolobaceae
Dimorphocerataceae
Gastriocerataceae
Goniatitaceae
Goniolobocerataceae
Marathonitaceae
Neodimorphocerataceae
Neoglyphiocerataceae
Neoicocerataceae
Nomismocerataceae
Pericyclaceae
Popanocerataceae
Schistocerataceae
Shumarditaceae
Somoholitaceae
Thalassocerataceae
