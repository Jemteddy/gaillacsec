St. Michael's Cathedral (Ukrainian: Михайлівський кафедральний собор) is an Orthodox church located in Cherkasy in central Ukraine. The church was constructed in 2000. It belongs to the Ukrainian Orthodox Church (Moscow Patriarchate). It is one of the biggest in Ukraine, and can hold up to 12,000 worshipers at one time.
