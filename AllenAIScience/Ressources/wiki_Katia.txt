Katia is a feminine given name. It is a variant of Katya. Katia may refer to the following people:
In music:
Katia Escalera, Bolivian soprano
Katia and Marielle Labèque, famous piano duo
Katia Ricciarelli, Italian soprano
In modelling:
Katia Zygouli, Greek model
In sports:
Katia Benth, French sprinting athlete
Kátia Cilene Teixeira da Silva, Brazilian female football (soccer) player
Kátia Lopes, Brazilian volleyball player
Katia Pisetsky, Israeli individual rhythmic gymnast born in the Ukraine
Katia Zini, Italian short track speed skater
In other fields:
Katia and Maurice Krafft, French volcanologists who died in a pyroclastic flow on Mount Unzen
Katia Kapovich, Russian poet now living in the United States
Katia Mann, wife of German writer Thomas Mann
Katia Sycara, a research professor in the Robotics Institute at Carnegie Mellon University
Katia Noyes, American author
In meteorology
Hurricane Katia, a powerful hurricane that caused over $150 million in damage in Europe
