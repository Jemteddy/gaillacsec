Yarumal is a municipality in the Antioquia Department, Colombia. Yarumal was founded in 1787 as San Luis de Gongora. Municipal status was granted in 1821. The current name of Yarumal comes from a local plant belonging to the Moraceae family, known in botanical Latin as Cecropia peltata L. The municipality (3 parishes and 20 villages) has an area of 724 kmÂ², 35,315 inhabitants, and its average elevation is 2,265 m above sea level.
Unusually many inhabitants suffer from early-onset Alzheimer's disease, caused by a genetic mutation.
