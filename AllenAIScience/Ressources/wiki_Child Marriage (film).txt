Child Marriage (Hindi: बाल विवाह) is an award-winning 2005 American film documentary about the Indian and Hindu practice of engaging children before they are born (see Child Marriage in India).


== Making ==
Film maker Neeraj Kumar traveled from Denver, Colorado (USA) to India, including stops in New Delhi, Rajasthan, and Haryana


== Awards and recognition ==
New York International Independent Film & Video Festival - Best Documentary
South Asian International Film Festival - Best Documentary
