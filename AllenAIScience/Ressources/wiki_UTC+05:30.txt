UTC+05:30 is an identifier for a time offset from UTC of +05:30. This time is used in India and Sri Lanka. It is 5 and a half hours ahead of Greenwich Mean Time or GMT.


== As standard time (all year round) ==


=== South Asia ===
India (New Delhi, Mumbai, Kolkata, Chennai) - Indian Standard Time
Sri Lanka - Sri Lanka Standard Time
