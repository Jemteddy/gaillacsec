Tajik National University is a national university of Tajikistan. It is located in Dushanbe.


== Notable alumni ==
Salimjon Aioubov - journalist, reporter and writer
Emomalii Rahmon - current president of Tajikistan
Umarali Quvvatov - an outspoken critic of veteran Tajik leader Emomali Rahmon
