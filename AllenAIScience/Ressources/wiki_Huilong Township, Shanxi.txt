Huilong Township (simplified Chinese: 回龙乡; traditional Chinese: 回龍鄉; pinyin: Huílóng Xiāng) is a township of Jiaokou County in southwestern Shanxi province, China, located 26 kilometres (16 mi) southeast of the county seat as the crow flies. As of 2011, it has ten villages under its administration.
