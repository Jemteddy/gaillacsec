Kačerginė is a small town 16 km (9.9 mi) west of Kaunas. The town of Kačerginė is located in Kaunas County, central Lithuania. Kačerginė was officially proclaimed a health resort in 1933. The only in Lithuania motor racing circuit Nemuno Žiedas was opened in 1960. As of 2001 it had a population of 715.
