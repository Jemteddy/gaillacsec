Anne One Hundred is a 1933 British drama film directed by Henry Edwards and starring Betty Stockfeld, Gyles Isham and Dennis Wyndham. It was based on the play Anne One Hundred Percent by Sewell Collins.


== Plot summary ==
A young woman inherits a soap factory from her father, and struggles to keep it open.
