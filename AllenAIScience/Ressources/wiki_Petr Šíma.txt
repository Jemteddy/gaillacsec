Petr Šíma (born 25 February 1983 in Domažlice) is a Czech football defender currently playing for FK Baník Sokolov, on loan from SK Dynamo České Budějovice.
Šíma played for the Czech Republic at the 2003 FIFA World Youth Championship in the United Arab Emirates.
