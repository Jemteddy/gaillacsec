The Tonnoidea are a superfamily of sea snails, marine gastropod molluscs in the clade Littorinimorpha. This superfamily includes many very large species.


== Families ==
Families within the superfamily Tonnoidea include:
Tonnidae, the tun shells
Bursidae, the frog shells
Laubierinidae
Personidae
Pisanianuridae
Ranellidae, the tritons
Cassidae, the helmet shells and bonnet shells
