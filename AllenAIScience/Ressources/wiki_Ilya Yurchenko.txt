Ilya Gennadyevich Yurchenko (Russian: Илья Геннадьевич Юрченко; born 9 April 1996) is a Russian football player who plays for FC Lokomotiv Liski.
He made his debut in the Russian Professional Football League for FC Lokomotiv Liski on 11 August 2015 in a game against FC Vityaz Podolsk.
