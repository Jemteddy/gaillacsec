Aizawl Monorail is the proposed Monorail for the town of Aizawl, capital of the state of Mizoram in North-East India. The decision for Monorail is taken because of increase in number of vehicles in town causing too much traffic problems.


== Network ==
There is only one line of length 5km running from north to the south of Aizawl.
Corridor (5km):- Zemabawk - Kulikawn


== Project Detail ==
Government of Mizoram has asked, Rail India Technical & Economic Services Limited (RITES), to prepare a technical as well as economic feasibility report of this mono rail system in Aizawl. The monorail project will be executed under Build, Own, Operate and Transfer(BOOT) basis.
