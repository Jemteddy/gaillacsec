Monastero di Lanzo is a comune (municipality) in the Province of Turin in the Italian region Piedmont, located about 35 km northwest of Turin.
Monastero di Lanzo borders the following municipalities: Locana, Cantoira, Coassolo Torinese, Ceres, Pessinetto, and Lanzo Torinese.
