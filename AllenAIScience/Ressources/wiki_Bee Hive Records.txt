Bee Hive Records was a jazz record label established in 1977 by Jim and Susan Neumann in Chicago.
In 2015, Mosaic Records released The Complete Bee Hive Sessions (Mosaic MD12-261), a twelve-disc box set consisting of sixteen Bee Hive recording sessions.
