Dorset & Wilts 3 South is an English Rugby Union league, forming part of the South West Division, for clubs based in northern Dorset. Promoted teams generally move up to Dorset & Wilts 2 South while relegated teams drop to Dorset & Wilts 4. In previous years Dorset & Wilts 3 had been one division with teams from Berkshire included as well as being split into three regional divisions - North, West and South - with Dorset & Wilts 3 West folding back in 2009, leaving just the two divisions.
