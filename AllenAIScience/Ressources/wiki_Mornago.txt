Mornago is a comune (municipality) in the Province of Varese, in the Italian region of Lombardy. It is located about 45 km northwest of Milan and about 10 km southwest of Varese. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 4,443 and an area of 12.3 km².
The municipality of Mornago contains the frazione (subdivision) of Crugnola Montonate Vinago.
Mornago borders the following municipalities: Arsago Seprio, Besnate, Casale Litta, Crosio della Valle, Sumirago, Vergiate.
