Dubovice is a village and municipality (obec) in Pelhřimov District in the Vysočina Region of the Czech Republic.
The municipality covers an area of 3.03 square kilometres (1.17 sq mi), and has a population of 63 (as at 2 October 2006).
Dubovice lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) west of Pelhřimov, 31 km (19 mi) west of Jihlava, and 91 km (57 mi) south-east of Prague.
