Circulation is an annual fire performance, juggling, and circus festival held in ÅŒtepoti/Dunedin, in the lower half of Te Wai Pounamu/ South Island of Aotearoa/New Zealand. Held every year since 2008, Circulation attracts members of the juggling community worldwide, hosting over 200 workshops, live music and djs, stand up comedy, fire performances and caters for children with family camping and Kidzone activities.
Workshops in past have covered a variety of topics from meditation, various forms of juggling, fire performance, object manipulation, poi, yoga and more.
