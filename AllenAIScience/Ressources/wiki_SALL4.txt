Sal-like protein 4 is a protein that in humans is encoded by the SALL4 gene.
Sal-like genes encode putative zinc finger transcription factors. For background information on SALL genes, see SALL1 (MIM 602218).[supplied by OMIM]
It can be associated with Duane-radial ray syndrome.
