"Sitting at the Wheel" is a 1983 hit single by The Moody Blues, written by John Lodge. It was first released on November 19, 1983, only one week after their previous single, "Blue World," was released. "Sitting at the Wheel" was later released on the Moody Blues 1983 album The Present, along with "Blue World." "Sitting at the Wheel" was a success for The Moody Blues, charting at #3 on the Mainstream Rock chart.
