Alicia Burley (born February 22, 1978, Lowell, Massachusetts, United States), is an American model. She was Playboy 's Cyber Girl of the Month for June 2003, and Cyber Girl of the Year for 2004.
Alicia stands 5'4". She measured 34D-23-33 and weighed 110 pounds when she was chosen as Playboy's Cyber Girl for the second week of February 2003. She later was promoted to Cyber Girl of the Month for June 2003, and eventually attained the title of 2004 Cyber Girl of the Year.
