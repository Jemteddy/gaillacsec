Kathiraon (Hindi: कठिरॉव) is a village in Pindra Tehsil of Varanasi district in the Indian state of Uttar Pradesh. Kathiraon has its own gram panchayat in the same name as the village. The village is about 45 kilometers North-West of Varanasi city, 269 kilometers South-East of state capital Lucknow and 778 kilometers South-East of the national capital Delhi.


== Demography ==
Kathiraon has a total population of 11,994 people amongst 1,823 families. Sex ratio of Kathiraon is 1,045 and child sex ratio is 910. Uttar Pradesh state average for both ratios is 912 and 902 respectively[note].


== Transportation ==
Kathiraon can be accessed by road and does not have a railway station of its own. Closest railway station to this village is Jalalganj railway station (14.0 kilometers North-East). Nearest operational airports are Varanasi airport (29.0 kilometers South-East) and Allahabad Airports (126 kilometers West).
