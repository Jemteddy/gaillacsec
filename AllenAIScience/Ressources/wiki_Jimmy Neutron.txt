Jimmy Neutron is a Nickelodeon franchise that involves a feature film, two television series, and several video games and television films.


== Jimmy Neutron: Boy Genius ==

Jimmy Neutron: Boy Genius is a 2001 animated film that was distributed by Paramount Pictures, and produced by Nickelodeon Movies with O Entertainment and DNA Productions. Directed by John A. Davis, the film centers on the parents of Jimmy's hometown being kidnapped by aliens known as Yokians, causing Jimmy and the rest of the children in town to band together to rescue them. The film received positive reviews.


== The Adventures of Jimmy Neutron: Boy Genius ==

The Adventures of Jimmy Neutron: Boy Genius is an American computer animated television series created by John A. Davis. It is the 2002 spin-off to the 2001 film. It originally aired on Nickelodeon for three seasons from July 20, 2002 to November 25, 2006.


== Planet Sheen ==

Planet Sheen is the second spin-off of Jimmy Neutron: Boy Genius. It centers on Jimmy's friend Sheen Estevez, who has accidentally landed on the planet Zeenu. It originally aired on Nickelodeon, and later remaining episodes on Nicktoons, for one season from October 2, 2010 to February 15, 2013.


== The Jimmy Timmy Power Hour ==

The Jimmy Timmy Power Hour is a series of made for TV films, which are crossovers with The Adventures of Jimmy Neutron: Boy Genius and The Fairly OddParents.


== Other television films ==
Other television films in the Jimmy Neutron series include The Egg-pire Strikes Back, Operation: Jet Fusion and Attack of the Twonkies.


== Video games ==

The Jimmy Neutron franchise has received several video games based on it, including Jimmy Neutron vs. Jimmy Negatron, The Adventures of Jimmy Neutron: Boy Genius: Attack of the Twonkies, and Jimmy Neutron: Boy Genius. In addition, Jimmy Neutron is a playable character in several other Nickelodeon video games, including Nicktoons: Attack of the Toybots, Nicktoons MLB, and Nicktoons: Freeze Frame Frenzy.
