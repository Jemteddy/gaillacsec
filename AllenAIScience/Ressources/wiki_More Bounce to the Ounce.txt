"More Bounce to the Ounce" is a song performed by American funk band Zapp. It is the opening track on their eponymous debut album and serves as the album's first single. The song was written and co-produced by band member Roger Troutman; and it peaked at #86 on the Billboard Hot 100 in 1980.
