Dalbergia maritima is a species of legume in the Fabaceae family; it is commonly known as rosewood. It is found only in Madagascar. It is threatened by habitat loss due to the over-consumption of its species.


== See also ==
Dalbergia baronii, also found only in Madagascar, and similarly threatened.
