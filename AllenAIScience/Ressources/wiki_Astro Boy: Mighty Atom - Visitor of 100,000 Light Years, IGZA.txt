Astro Boy: Mighty Atom - Visitor of 100,000 Light Years, IGZA (アストロボーイ・鉄腕アトム１０万光年の来訪者・イグザ, Asutoro Bōi: Tetsuwan Atomu - Jū-man Kōnen no Raihō-sha, Iguza))(shortened to Astro Boy vs IGZA) is a 2005 Japanese anime science fiction adventure film based on the 2003 Astro Boy TV series made by Tezuka Productions and distributed by Sarai Inc. It was released on September 1, 2005. The movie has only been shown in Japan, and has never been planned or carried out in English.
