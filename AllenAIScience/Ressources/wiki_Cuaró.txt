Cuaró is a caserío (hamlet) in the sparsely populated central Artigas Department of northern Uruguay.


== Location ==
It is located about 23 kilometres (14 mi) northwest of Paso Campamento and about 23 kilometres (14 mi) southwest of Javier de Viana.


== Population ==
In 2011 Cuaró had a population of 113.
Source: Instituto Nacional de Estadística de Uruguay
