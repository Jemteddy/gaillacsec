The Niko Dovana Stadium is a multi-use stadium in Durrës, Albania. It is currently used mostly for football matches and is the home ground of KS Teuta. The stadium has a capacity of 12,040 and was renamed in the early 1990s in honour of former Teuta Durrës goalkeeper and financier Niko Dovana.
The Albania national football team played in the Niko Dovana Stadium for the first time in a friendly international match against Uzbekistan on 11 August 2010.
