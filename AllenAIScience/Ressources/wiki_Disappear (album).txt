Disappear is the seventh studio album by the American hardcore punk band T.S.O.L. (True Sounds of Liberty), released in 2001 through Nitro Records.


== Track listing ==
All songs written and composed by Jack Grisham, Ron Emory, Mike Roche, and Jay O'Brien. 


== Personnel ==
Band
Jack Grisham – vocals, piano
Ron Emory – guitar; lead vocals on "In My Head"
Mike Roche – bass guitar
Jay O'Brien – drums
Additional musicians
Pat O'Leary – backing vocals on "In My Head"
Alan Morphew – trumpet on "Socialite"
Production
Thom Wilson – producer, recording engineer
Kris Stencel – layout and design
