Western Economic Diversification Canada (WD) is a Canadian federal department that works towards building strong, competitive and innovative businesses and communities in Western Canada. Their main priorities focus on community economic development; trade and investment; innovation; and business development.
The Minister of Western Economic Diversification is Rona Ambrose, and the Minister of State of Western Economic Diversification is Michelle Rempel.
WD's headquarters is located in Edmonton, with regional offices located in British Columbia, Saskatchewan, and Manitoba; a liaison office in Ottawa; and a regional satellite office in Calgary.
