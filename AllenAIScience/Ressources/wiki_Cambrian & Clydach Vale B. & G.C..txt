Cambrian & Clydach Vale Boys & Girls Club is a Welsh football club based in Clydach Vale in the Rhondda Valley. After playing their football in the South Wales Amateur League for a number of years, they joined the Welsh Football League in 2005, and have risen through the divisions by finishing in promotion positions in Divisions Three and Two in successive seasons and are currently members of Division One.
